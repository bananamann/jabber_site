Connect to the world around you with buzzl

buzzl is your local information source. Location-based posts allow you to stay connected and informed like never before. Make new friends, find great deals, expand your professional network, or just find out when happy hour starts.

A local information and connection platform

With global news easily available, people often know more about events in other cities or countries before they know what's happening down the street.


buzzl is here to change that.

How it Works


All posts within buzzl are tied to a physical location (don't worry we never share your location with others)

You can view other users' posts within a specified radius. Perfect for connecting to the people and businesses around you!
